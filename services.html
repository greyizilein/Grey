Grey offers structured investment opportunities in Nigeria's most in-demand agricultural commodities. By investing with us, you participate in the food supply chain — buying staple crops at harvest-low prices and selling at market-high rates as demand rises.

We source directly from farming cooperatives, cutting out middlemen and securing reliable supply chains that protect your capital. You don't manage storage or logistics — Grey handles everything.

Free Consultation →

How It Works

Step 01
Choose Your Commodity

Select from our range of staple crops — Maize, Rice, Millet, Sorghum, or Soybeans — each priced at farm-gate value and tied to consistent market demand.

Step 02
Decide Your Units

Each unit represents a measurable portion of your chosen commodity. Start with one unit or commit to dozens. Scale as your confidence grows.

Step 03
Select Your Duration

Choose a 3, 6, 9, or 12-month cycle based on your liquidity needs. Longer cycles yield higher returns as commodities appreciate further.

Step 04
Receive Your Returns

At maturity, Grey pays back your full capital plus all accrued profit. You may also reinvest for compounding growth into the next cycle.
